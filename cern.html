Assisted Teleoperation of Kinova Arm for Remote Maintenance in Hazardous Environment

Short Term Research Internship at EN-SMM-MRO, CERN, Geneva, Switzerland

Project Guide - Mr. Mario Di Castro, Giacomo Lunghi, Dr. V Laxmi

CERN is a nuclear research centre which uses robots for maintenance, control repair and inspection of radioactive substances. Problem : It takes 72 hours for manual teleoperation of 50 km tunnel.

Aim: Reduce time of manual teleoperation by developing a semi-autonomous system which can be controlled remotely. Approach: Given the human initiates the trajctory of the robotic arm, the arm will be able to complete the rest of the trajectory on its own using "Prediction of Motion Primitives"
