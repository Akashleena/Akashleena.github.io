Hi! This is Akashleena (aka Aleena)

I'm currently working as a Path Planning and Algorithms Engineer , in an aerospace startup, Newspace Research and Technologies, Bengaluru, India. My research interests are SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping), autonomous navigation of mobile robots and UAVs, Human Robot Interaction and and Control Theory. Let's start scrolling and learn more about me.

About Me

Learning gives creativity. Creativity leads to thinking, Thinking leads to knowledge, Knowledge makes you great. - Dr APJ. Abdul Kalam

I was introduced to this creative field of robotics in the first year of my undergrads when I was selected to be a part of Robolution, the robotics club of my university Birla Institute of Technology Mesra, India. I completed my Bachelor's in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from BIT Mesra, India in 2020.

My long term career goal is to pursue a research oriented profession which can solve real life problems and cater to the industry. In this pursuit, I am striving hard to develop strong fundamentals in mathematics, motion planning, localization and controls. I would like to see myself as a RoboArtist, who is not just confined to the software side but also knows how to build and prototype fast.

At a personal level, I am a creative person who has a wide range of hobbies loves dancing, mathematics, solving puzzles, travelling, reading printed books, writing blogs and Photography. Head on to the "Non-Technical interests" section to know more about my work in this area.
